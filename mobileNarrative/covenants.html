Covenants and Their Rewards

Co-op Covenants focus on friendly interactions between players, where after defeating a boss both players are rewarded.

PvP Covenants are all about invasions, where one player will join another's world to hunt them down for a reward.

The Yellow boxes represent the Co-op covenants, while every other box is a PvP covenant with unique rewards assosiated with them.
